a. Expected number of years of patent life for a new drug

$\{\sigma^2 = E[({X-\mu})^2] =  \Sigma({{x_i}-\mu})^2p({x_i})\approx 4.73\}$

$\{\sigma = \sqrt{\Sigma({{x_i}-\mu})^2p({x_i})}\approx 2.175\}$

$\{\mu-2\sigma\approx 3.55\}$ 
$\{\mu+2\sigma\approx 12.25\}$
