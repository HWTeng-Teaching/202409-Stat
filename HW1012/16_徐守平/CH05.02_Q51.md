a. 

$P(x \geq 5) = 1-P(x \\leq 4) = 1- .902 = .098$
- - -
b. 

$P(x \\leq 6) = .991$
- - - 
c. 

$P(x > 4) = 1 - P(x \\leq 4) = 1 - .902 = .098$
- - - 

d. 

$P(x = 4) = P(x \\leq 4) - P(x \\leq 3) = .902 -.764 = .138$
- - -

$P(3 \\leq x \\leq 5) = P(x \\leq 5) - P(x \\leq 2) = .967 -.537 = .430$
