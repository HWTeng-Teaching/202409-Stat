### a. $\mu =176.5cm \text {\ and \} \sigma =8.9cm$ :   
Trump is 188cm, standardize it by z-score :  

$\{
z-score = \frac{188-176.5}{8.9}=1.292
\}$ 

$\{
z=1.292 < 2 
\}$ 

$\{
z值小於2，得知 Trump 的身高在美國並沒有異常的高。
\}$
