### b.Mean, Variance, Standard deviation : 

(1) Mean : 
$\{\mu = E[X] = \Sigma{x_i}p({x_i})
\=7
\}$ 

(2) Variance : 
$\{\sigma^2 = E[({X-\mu})^2] =\Sigma({{x_i}-\mu})^2p({x_i})
\approx 5.833
\}$  

(3) Standard deviation : 
$\{\sigma = \sqrt{\Sigma({{x_i}-\mu})^2p({x_i})}
\approx 2.415
\}$
