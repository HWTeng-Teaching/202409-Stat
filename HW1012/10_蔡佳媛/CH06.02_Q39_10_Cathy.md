### $\mu = 1.2$ and $\sigma =0.15$ Find $P(1.00 \lt x \lt 1.10)$

$X \sim Normal(\mu = 1.2, \sigma = 0.15)$

$P(1 \lt x \lt 1.1)$
$=P(\frac{1-1.2}{0.15} \lt x \lt \frac{1.1-1.2}{0.15})$
$=P(-1.33 \lt x \lt -0.67) = 0.2514 - 0.0918 = 0.1596$
