$\mu = \sum (x \cdot P(E)) = 2*{\frac {1}{36}}+3*{\frac {2}{36}}+......+12*{\frac {1}{36}}=7.0$


$\sigma^2 = \sum (x^2 \cdot P(E)) - \mu^2 = (2^2*{\frac {1}{36}}-7^2)+(3^2*{\frac {2}{36}}-7^2)+......+(12^2*{\frac {1}{36}}-7^2) = 5.83$

$\sigma = \sqrt{\sigma^2} = \sqrt{5.83} = 2.42 $
