### Mean (μ): 7

$\mu = E[X] = \sum x_i p(x_i) = 7$

### Variance (σ²): 5.8333

$\sigma^2 = E[(X - \mu)^2] = \sum (x_i - \mu)^2 p(x_i) \approx 5.8333$

### Standard Deviation (σ): 2.4151

$\sigma = \sqrt{\sigma^2} \approx 2.4151$
