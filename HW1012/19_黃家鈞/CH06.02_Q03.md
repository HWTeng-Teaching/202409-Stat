P(-2.33 < z < 2.33) = P(z<2.33) - P(z<-2.33)

查表得

P(z<2.33) = 1 - P(z<-2.33) = 1 - 0.0099 = 0.9901

0.9901 - 0.0099 = 0.9802

or

P(-2.33 < z < 2.33) 
= [0.5 - P(z < -2.33)] * 2 

= (0.5 - 0.0099) * 2 = 0.9802
