X ~ Binomial (n =100, p = .2), X = 1, 2, 3, ... 100

$Binominal(n,p) \approx N(\mu = np, \sigma^2 = npq),q=1-p$ 

$\\mu = 100 \times 0.2 = 20\$

$\\sigma = \sqrt{100 \times 0.2 \times (1 - 0.2)} = \sqrt{16} = 4\$
