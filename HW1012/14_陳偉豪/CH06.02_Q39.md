$\mu=1.2 \quad \sigma=0.15 \quad P(1.00<x<1.10)$  
Standardized:  
$P(1.00<x<1.10) =P(\frac {1.00-\mu}{\sigma}<z<\frac {1.10-\mu}{\sigma}) = P(\frac {1.00-1.2}{0.15}<z<\frac {1.10-1.2}{0.15}) =P(-1.33<z<-0.67)= 0.2514-0.0918 = 0.1596$
