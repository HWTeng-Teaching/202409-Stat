### c. Are there any outliers?

<br/>

$IQR = 317.5 - 130.5 = 187$  
$IQR\times1.5 = 187\times1.5=280.5$  
$130.5 - 280.5 = (150) $   
$317.5 + 280 = 598$  

<br/>

因所有數據均在(150)到598的範圍內，因此判斷無outliers
