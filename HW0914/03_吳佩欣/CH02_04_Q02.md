# CH02_04_Q02

## 吳佩欣(513717003)

### Solution

Mean: 3.51
$$
\bar{x} = \frac{\sum{x_i}}{n}
$$


Standard deviation: 2.06
$$
s = \sqrt{\frac{\sum{(x_i - \bar{x})^2}}{n - 1}}
$$


Z-scores corresponding to minimun and maximum: 
Max 7.8
Z-score = (7.8 - 3.51)/2.06 = 2.08

Min 1.0
Z-score = (1.0 - 3.51)/2.06 = -1.22

不到3個標準差, 顯示不太有outliers.
