## Answer  
2. n=8 measurements: 3, 2, 5, 6, 4, 4, 3, 5

$$ \quad Mean = \frac{\Sigma X~{i}~} {n} $$

$$ \quad Mean = \frac{(3+2+5+6+4+4+3+5)}{8}=4 $$ 

---

$$ \quad \ Median = \frac{(8+1)}{2}=4.5^{th} $$ 

重新排列後數列為2, 3, 3, 4, 4, 5, 5, 6  

$$ \quad \ Median = 4 $$

---

$$\quad \ Mode = 3,4,5 $$
