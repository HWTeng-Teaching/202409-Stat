## Answer 

Sample Space

$$P_2^5 = \frac{5!}{(5-2)!}=5\times4=20$$

$$P(Orange) = \frac{1}{5} $$

$$P(Not Red) = \frac{3}{4} $$

$$P(\text{One for Orange and One for not Red}) = \frac{1}{5}\times\frac{3}{4} = \frac{3}{20} $$
