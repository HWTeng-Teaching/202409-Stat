### a. Find $p(1)$, $p(2)$, and $p(3)$

$p(1) = \frac{1}{10} = 0.1$  

$p(2) = \frac{9}{10} \times \frac{1}{10} = 0.09$  

$p(3) = \frac{9}{10} \times \frac{9}{10} \times \frac{1}{10} = 0.081$  

<br/>

### b. Give a formula for $p(x)$

$p(x) = ({\frac{9}{10}})^{n-1} \times \frac{1}{10}$
