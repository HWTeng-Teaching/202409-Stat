>$S_x = \sqrt{\frac{\sum x^2 - \left(\frac{\sum x}{n}\right)^2}{n - 1}}$
>
>$S_y = \sqrt{\frac{\sum y^2 - \left(\frac{\sum y}{n}\right)^2}{n - 1}}$
>
>$S_{xy} = \frac{\sum xy - \frac{\sum x \sum y}{n}}{n - 1}$
>
>$r = \frac{S_{xy}}{S_x S_y}$
>
>$b = r \times \frac{S_y}{S_x}$
