### Calculate the width of the confidence interval for $\mu$ for the confidence level 99% ($\alpha$ = 0.01)

$\bar{X} \pm z_{\frac{\alpha}{2}} \frac{s}{\sqrt{n}} = \bar{x} \pm z_{0.005} \frac{10}{\sqrt{100}} = \bar{x} \pm 2.58 \times 1 = \bar{x} \pm 1$
