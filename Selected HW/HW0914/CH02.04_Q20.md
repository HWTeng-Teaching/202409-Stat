### c. Are there any outliers?
1. **Calculate the IQR**:\
IQR=Q3−Q1=317.5-130.5=187
2. **To determine if there are outliers, we can use the Interquartile Range (IQR) method:**\
Lower bound: Q1−1.5×IQR\
130.5-(1.5×187)=-150\
Upper bound: Q3+1.5×IQR\
317.5+(1.5×187)=-598
3. **Outliers Analysis**:\
Lower Bound: -150\
Upper Bound: 598\
